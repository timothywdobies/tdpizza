❌ What NOT to Buy (Save Your Money)

❌ Countertop Pizza Ovens ($200-400)

Your apartment oven works fine with the right technique. These take up counter space and don't solve the real problem (understanding heat management).

❌ Pizza Stones (Ceramic/Cordierite)

They crack. They're fragile. Steel is better in every way (faster heat-up, more durable, better conductivity).

❌ Fancy Dough Mixers

You're making 1-4 pizzas, not a bakery. Your hands + a bowl work perfectly. Don't waste $300 on a stand mixer for pizza dough.

❌ "00" Flour (Until You're Advanced)

Bread flour works great in apartment ovens. 00 flour is for 900°F ovens. You don't have that. Save the $15/bag.
