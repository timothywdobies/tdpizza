Frequently Asked Questions

What format is the Playbook?

It's a 28-page PDF that works on any device. Download it instantly after purchase. Print it or keep it on your phone/tablet while you cook.

Do I need special equipment?

Nope. You need a ~$30 starter kit: digital scale, sheet pan, and instant-read thermometer. The Playbook covers both sheet-pan pizza (beginner) and pizza steel (optional upgrade). Your standard apartment oven works fine.

Will this work in MY oven?

Yes. This system is designed specifically for weak apartment ovens (electric or gas). If your oven goes to 500°F and has a broiler, you're good to go.

What if it doesn't work for me?

30-day money-back guarantee. If you follow the system and still can't make good pizza, email me and I'll refund you immediately. No questions, no hassle.

How long does it take to make pizza?

Same-day dough: 6 hours total (5 min active work). Overnight cold-ferment: Mix the night before, bake the next day. Once your dough is ready, baking takes 15-20 minutes.

Is this just a recipe?

No. It's a complete system with formulas, timing charts, troubleshooting flowcharts, visual guides, and a tiny-kitchen workflow. You get the WHY behind every step, not just ingredients and instructions.

Absolutely. One purchase = household use. Just don't post it publicly online.
