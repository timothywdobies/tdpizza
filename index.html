Frequently Asked Questions

What format is it?

PDF. 28 pages. Download right after you buy. You can print it or just keep it on your phone while you're cooking.

Do I need special equipment?

Nope. Just a $15 kitchen scale, a sheet pan (you probably have one), and a cheap thermometer. The playbook shows you the sheet pan method first, then how to upgrade to a pizza steel later if you want.

Will this work in my oven?

If your oven goes to 500°F and has a broiler, yeah. This was made for normal apartment ovens that suck.

What if it doesn't work?

Email me within 30 days and I'll refund you. No questions.

How long does it take?

Same-day version takes 6 hours start to finish (but you're only working for like 10 minutes total). Overnight version: mix it the night before, bake it the next day. Baking itself is 15-20 minutes.

Is this just a recipe?

No. It's the recipe plus timing charts, troubleshooting guides, photos of what the dough should look like, and how to set up your kitchen so it's not a mess. Basically everything you need to know.

Can I share it?

Yeah, with your roommate or partner. Just don't post it online.

Who Made This?

I'm not a chef. I rent an apartment with a mediocre oven and about 8 square feet of counter space. I spent two years figuring out how to make actually good pizza without any of the gear or space that normal recipes assume you have.

This is just everything I learned, written down. No fluff, no expensive equipment you don't need. Just what works.
